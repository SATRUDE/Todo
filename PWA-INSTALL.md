# Installing the Todo App as a PWA

Your Todo app is now a Progressive Web App (PWA)! You can install it on your phone or computer and it will work like a native app without showing the browser UI.

## How to Install

### On iPhone/iPad (Safari)

1. Open Safari and go to your deployed app URL
2. Tap the **Share** button (square with arrow) at the bottom
3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Customize the name if you want (default: "Todo")
5. Tap **"Add"**
6. The app icon will appear on your home screen!

### On Android (Chrome)

1. Open Chrome and go to your deployed app URL
2. Tap the **menu** (three dots) in the top right
3. Tap **"Add to Home screen"** or **"Install app"**
4. Tap **"Install"** in the popup
5. The app will be installed and can be opened from your app drawer!

### On Desktop (Chrome/Edge)

1. Open Chrome or Edge and go to your deployed app URL
2. Look for the **install icon** in the address bar (or a popup will appear)
3. Click **"Install"** or the install icon
4. The app will open in its own window without browser UI!
